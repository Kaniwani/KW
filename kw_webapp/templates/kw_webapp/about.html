What is KaniWani?

Is a companion website to WaniKani, an SRS tool for kanji and vocabulary memorization. I found that WaniKani helped a lot with being able to recognize and read Kanji, but really didn't help that much in trying to recall words without the visual stimulation of the Kanji. KaniWani attempts to bridge that gap by using your WaniKani information and force you to do the opposite of what you normally do.

Moving Forward

Kaniwani starts off at your current WK level, so you don't have to go through all the previous levels unless you want to.

Unlocking the past

Manually unlock previous levels to add their vocabulary to your review schedule.
